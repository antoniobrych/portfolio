Mike Wazowski

Michael "Mike" Thomas Wazowski is the deuteragonist of the 2001 Disney Pixar animated film Monsters, Inc. He is also the protagonist of its 2013 prequel, Monsters University. Depicted as a diminutive, one-eyed monster with a wisecracking veneer, Mike is an employee of Monsters, Incorporated, where he works closely with his longtime partner/best friend Sulley.
